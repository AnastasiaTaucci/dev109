Disc Golf

Disc golf is similar to traditional golf, but instead of using clubs and balls, players use specialized discs and aim for a disc golf basket. The basket consists of a pole with chains and a basket to catch the disc. The objective of the game is to complete each hole with the fewest number of throws, starting from a designated tee area and ending when the disc comes to rest in the basket.
